Locations in North America and Pacific Islands

Location: Anchorage, Alaska

I really enjoyed going to Alaska when I was in elementary school so I want to go again. The glaciers were amazing and there was lots of wild life like whales and eagles. Also there were many amazing wooden sculptures like totem poles.

Location: Maui, Hawaii

Hawaii is quite an obvious choice for place to go. A place for relaxation and fun. I enjoy all the swimming and beaches. The sunsets always amaze me as well.

Location: New York, New York

I have never been to New York nut I want to. The amazing skyscrapers, Broadway, and Central Park should make a good visit to start at.
